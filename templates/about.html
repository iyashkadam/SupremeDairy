About Us

We are a rapidly growing public limited company, headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Incorporated in 2020, we began our production journey in 2022. Since then, we've become one of the fastest-growing public-listed companies in Pune, with a commitment to excellence in everything we do.

Our Mission

At the core of our operations is a mission to embrace cutting-edge technology to delight our customers, empower the farming community through our unique 'Relationship Farming' model, and consistently meet the ever-changing needs of the market.

We are committed to creating high-quality products and ensuring their availability through innovative and convenient channels, making us a trusted partner for our customers.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as the most reliable and customer-centric company in the industry, revolutionizing dairy products by utilizing modern technology, sustainability, and fostering long-term relationships with farmers and customers alike.
